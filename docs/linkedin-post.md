🚀 I just shipped my first startup as an MS CS student at ASU.

It's called **LocalPulse** — an AI-powered business intelligence platform built specifically for small businesses in Tempe, AZ.

Here's the problem I'm solving:
→ Thousands of local restaurants, salons, and shops on Mill Ave have zero data about their customers
→ They're leaving money on the table every Tuesday afternoon
→ They can't afford $500/hr consultants or enterprise software

So I built them a $299/month platform that gives them:

📊 Real-time dashboard (Google + Yelp reviews, foot traffic, ratings)
🤖 AI that auto-drafts responses to every new review
💡 Weekly AI reports: "Your slowest day is Tuesday — here's a promo idea"
📱 Automated SMS campaigns to customers during slow hours

The whole stack: Node.js + Express backend, Supabase for data, OpenAI GPT-4o for insights, n8n for automations, Twilio for SMS.

I built this because I walk past these businesses every day and I know they're struggling with things that a CS student can fix with a weekend of code.
